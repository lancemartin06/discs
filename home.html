About DIOSCAS

            We are a local organization that has a incredible passion for disc golf. 
            Not only do we have a passion for disc golf, but for the community as well. 
            
            With that, we have spent many hours collecting lost discs from 
            some pretty unfriendly ponds here in Boise. We don't charge for 
            recovery, and you can find your lost discs here.
